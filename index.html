What We Do

Three disciplines. One standard of rigor.

We concentrate deliberately on a narrow set of high-stakes padel mandates rather than spreading thin across the broader sports industry. Every engagement is led by senior advisors, not delegated to junior staff.

◆

Club Investment & Development

We advise investors and operators on the feasibility, business planning and development of padel clubs, franchises and multi-court complexes — from site selection through to opening.

◆

M&A & Due Diligence

We lead buy-side and sell-side advisory for club and chain transactions — commercial, financial and operational due diligence for buyers, sellers and investors across borders.

◆

Revenue & Occupancy Optimization

For operating clubs, we diagnose court utilization, pricing and membership models to lift revenue and occupancy without new capital investment.
