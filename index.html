What We Do

Two disciplines. One standard of rigor.

We concentrate deliberately on a narrow set of high-stakes mandates rather than spreading thin across the sports industry. Every engagement is led by senior advisors, not delegated to junior staff.

◆

M&A & Transaction Due Diligence

We advise buyers, sellers and investors on the acquisition, sale and valuation of clubs, franchises, media rights and sports-related assets — leading commercial, financial and operational due diligence across borders and regulatory regimes.

◆

Infrastructure & Facility Development

From stadiums and training academies to multi-use sports complexes, we lead feasibility studies, master planning and delivery oversight for capital-intensive infrastructure projects on behalf of owners, federations and public partners.
