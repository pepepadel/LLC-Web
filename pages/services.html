Services

Two disciplines, delivered with senior-level rigor.

Bluesport Consulting concentrates its practice on two areas where the financial stakes, complexity and reputational risk of international sport are highest.

Practice 01

M&A & Transaction Due Diligence

We advise ownership groups, investors and institutions through the full lifecycle of sports transactions — from initial strategy to closing and post-transaction integration.

01

Buy-side & sell-side advisory

Strategy, target identification, valuation and negotiation support for the acquisition or divestment of clubs, franchises and sports assets.

02

Commercial & financial due diligence

Independent assessment of revenue quality, contractual exposure, media and sponsorship rights, and financial sustainability ahead of a transaction.

03

Regulatory & governance review

Navigating league, federation and cross-border ownership rules to de-risk approval processes for prospective owners and investors.

04

Valuation & deal structuring

Objective valuation frameworks and structuring guidance aligned to the client's long-term ownership or exit strategy.

Practice 02

Infrastructure & Facility Development

We guide owners, federations and public partners through the planning and delivery of capital-intensive sports infrastructure, from feasibility through to operational readiness.

01

Feasibility & site strategy

Market, financial and site feasibility studies for stadiums, training academies and multi-use sports complexes.

02

Master planning & funding strategy

Development of master plans and capital strategies that align public, private and institutional funding sources.

03

Delivery & project oversight

Independent oversight of design and construction delivery, protecting owner interests through to completion.

04

Operational readiness

Commercial and operating model design so new facilities are positioned to perform from day one.
