Services

Three disciplines, delivered with senior-level rigor.

Padel4Rent Consulting concentrates its practice on three areas where the financial stakes, complexity and growth potential of the international padel market are highest.

Practice 01

Club Investment & Development

We guide investors and operators through the planning and launch of padel clubs and multi-court complexes, from initial feasibility to opening day.

01

Market & site feasibility

Demand analysis, catchment modeling and site selection for new padel clubs and court expansions.

02

Business planning & funding strategy

Development of business plans and capital strategies that align investors, operators and lenders around a single model.

03

Franchise & multi-site strategy

Structuring for operators expanding across multiple clubs or territories, including franchise and licensing models.

04

Delivery & opening readiness

Independent oversight from construction through to commercial and operational readiness for launch.

Practice 02

M&A & Due Diligence

We advise ownership groups, investors and chains through the full lifecycle of padel transactions — from initial strategy to closing and post-transaction integration.

01

Buy-side & sell-side advisory

Strategy, target identification, valuation and negotiation support for the acquisition or divestment of clubs and chains.

02

Commercial & financial due diligence

Independent assessment of revenue quality, membership base, lease exposure and financial sustainability ahead of a transaction.

03

Cross-border & regulatory review

Navigating federation, licensing and cross-border ownership considerations to de-risk approval processes.

04

Valuation & deal structuring

Objective valuation frameworks and structuring guidance aligned to the client's long-term ownership or exit strategy.

Practice 03

Revenue & Occupancy Optimization

For operating clubs, we diagnose and improve court utilization, pricing and membership economics — without requiring new capital investment.

01

Court utilization diagnostics

Data-driven review of booking patterns, peak-hour saturation and off-peak demand to identify revenue leakage.

02

Pricing & yield strategy

Dynamic pricing, membership tiering and package design calibrated to local demand elasticity.

03

Ancillary revenue development

Coaching, retail, F&B and events programs designed to lift revenue per court beyond bookings alone.

04

Operating model & benchmarking

Performance benchmarking against comparable clubs and markets to set realistic, defensible targets.
